Brain Tumor Detection AI

Create an AI system that can process and analyze MRI scan images of the brain to detect the presence of brain tumors. The system should perform the following tasks:

  • Load and interpret MRI scan images (preferably in DICOM format or other common formats like JPEG or PNG).
  • Use advanced image processing techniques, such as convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to detect anomalies, abnormalities, or masses in the brain tissue.
  • Identify and classify any detected tumors into categories such as benign, malignant, or inconclusive, based on the image characteristics and size of the tumor.
  • Provide a confidence score for each detection to indicate the likelihood that the detected abnormality is a tumor.
  • Display the results with visual annotations on the MRI scan, highlighting the location of any detected tumors or abnormalities.
  • Ensure that the system has high accuracy and sensitivity, minimizing false negatives (tumors that are missed) and false positives (misidentified non-tumor areas).
  • Optionally, generate a summary report with additional details, such as the type of tumor (if identified), potential treatment suggestions, or a recommendation for further medical consultation.
